South End Consulting LLC started in Boston, MA as a web development firm. Over the years, the expertise we have amassed in the field of selling products online has evolved our business from a pure software development company into a hybrid technology provider/marketing firm.

South End Consulting focuses on small to mid-sized retail or wholesale operations needing enterprise solutions for their online sale, distribution, inventory, and marketing efforts. Our team of developers have weathered the craziness of the Dot.com era and use their experience to bring high end business solutions to the small business.

We are committed to providing our clients with the tools and flexibility to make online commerce seamless and efficient. We work with existing business applications and logic to extend control of the website to traditional POS/Inventory Control systems, and into the warehouse.
